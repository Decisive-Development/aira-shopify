# Shipping & Delivery

We take the same care getting your order to you as we did designing what's inside it.

We deliver across the UAE and Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Bahrain, and Oman.

## Tracking

Once your order is on its way, you'll get a tracking link by SMS or email, so you always know exactly where it is.

---

## Customs & duties (GCC)

All GCC orders are shipped Delivered Duty Paid (DDP). That means customs and taxes are sorted on our end - no surprise charges when your order arrives.

---

## Your delivery address

Double-check your address and phone number before placing your order. If something's off, it can delay your delivery and we won't be able to reroute it once it's dispatched.

---

## If something goes wrong

Every order is guaranteed. If yours doesn't arrive for reasons outside our control, we'll send a replacement to the same address — no back and forth.
