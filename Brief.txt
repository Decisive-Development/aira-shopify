=====================================================================
These OVERRIDE the designer's wording wherever they conflict. The designer
doesn't have our locked standards; we do.

- CLAIMS: Every claim must be mechanically true, sourced, or user-reported.
  NOTHING invented. NO fake review counts ("1,200+ breathers"), NO fake
  testimonial totals ("10,000+ people"), NO "clinically designed".
  Use: real beta quotes (collect this week) + the sourced UAE snoring stat.
- PLAN DEFAULT: One-time purchase stays DEFAULT-selected at launch.
  Do NOT promote Subscribe & Save to priority/default (designer suggested
  this; we've decided against it for an unknown brand. Revisit with data).
- UNITS: pairs, never tabs (1 pair = 1 night). Sizes L1 to L4.
- CTA: "Add to cart" (this is a PDP). Sentence case. ("Get my kit" is the
  HOMEPAGE CTA only.)
- BRAND CASING: "aira" lowercase always, even sentence-initial.
- GUARANTEE: 30-NIGHT guarantee. Say "nights", never "days".
- SHIPPING: "Free shipping across the GCC." + delivery timeframe.
  >> TOM TO CONFIRM real working-day range before this ships. <<
- COPY RULES: UK English. Sentence case everywhere incl. buttons/badges.
  ALL CAPS only for eyebrows. NO em-dashes, hyphens-as-pivots, or arrows
  in customer copy (reads as AI). Read-aloud test is law.
- SKU: Kit ships 15 pairs. "What's in the box" must reflect the real
  count. >> TOM TO CONFIRM 15 vs 30 before "month" language is used. <<
