## Return & Refund Policy

**Our Promise**

We stand behind every product we make. If you're not satisfied with your AIRA experience, we're here to make it right.

**30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee**

Try AIRA for 30 days. If you're not happy with your purchase for any reason, contact us at contact@aira-breathing.com within 30 days of delivery and we'll guide you through the return process.

---

**Magnetic Strip Returns**

The reusable magnetic strip is eligible for return within 30 days of delivery, provided it is in good physical condition, not broken or damaged and returned in its original packaging.

To initiate a return, simply contact our support team with your order number. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer.

---

**Adhesive Tabs Returns**

Adhesive tab packs are eligible for return only if they are completely unopened and untouched. Once a pack has been opened or a tab has been removed, it cannot be returned or refunded. This applies to both individual purchases and subscription refill orders.

We encourage you to try the tabs, they're designed to be used.

---

**Subscription Refill Plan**

You may pause, skip, or cancel your refill subscription at any time through your account portal. Changes must be made at least 72 hours before your next billing date to avoid being charged for that cycle.

Orders that have already been processed and shipped cannot be cancelled but may be returned unopened within 30 days, with return shipping at the customer's expense.

---

**Important Notes**

Refunds are only available for purchases made directly through our official website. If you purchased through a third-party platform, please initiate your return through that platform directly.

Refunds will be processed within 5 to 10 business days of receiving your return.

---

**A note from us**

We're a small brand that genuinely cares about your experience. If something isn't working for you, reach out before you request a return we'd love the chance to help first.
